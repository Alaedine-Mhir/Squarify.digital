Bubble is a no-code platform that lets you build web apps without needing to know how to code. It's been around for a while, with millions of users and solid backing. With Bubble, you can design, build, and launch apps all in one place.
With Bubble, you can create almost any web app you can think of. You handle everything from design, data storage, logic, and even add third-party services like payment gateways or APIs. Basically, you can build full-fledged apps without ever writing code.
Yes, Bubble is secure. It uses HTTPS encryption, passes data through Cloudflare, and hosts everything on Amazon Web Services (AWS). It also has strong user authentication features, and it's compliant with industry standards like GDPR and SOC 2.
Absolutely. Bubble is built to scale, but it depends on how well you set up your app. It gives you tools to track performance and make adjustments as needed. You can also add extra server resources without upgrading your plan, making it easy to grow as your app does.
There are a few things Bubble can't handle, like offline mobile apps, real-time money transfers (for example, stock or crypto trading), and exporting your app's code. But for most regular web apps, Bubble works perfectly fine.
